1. Introduction

Welcome to HarshGuruJi (accessible via https://webguruji.online). We are an educational and free tool platform dedicated to providing accessible learning resources, interactive Web API utilities, and AI assistance through GuruAI.

This Cookie Policy explains how and why cookies, web beacons, local storage (`localStorage`, `sessionStorage`), and similar technologies are used when you visit or interact with our platform.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data text files that websites store on your computer, smartphone, or browser when you visit a webpage. They help websites remember your device, preferences, login state, and active settings over time.

In addition to traditional HTTP cookies, modern web platforms utilize HTML5 LocalStorage and SessionStorage to store client-side state data locally in your browser without transmitting sensitive headers on every HTTP call.

3. Types of Cookies Used

We classify the storage mechanisms used across HarshGuruJi into the following categories:

🔒 Session Cookies

Temporary storage cleared automatically as soon as you close your browser session.

💾 Persistent Cookies

Stored locally on your device for a designated expiration duration to remember preferences.

⚡ First-Party Cookies

Set directly by HarshGuruJi domain (webguruji.online) to handle core site features.

🌐 Third-Party Cookies

Set by external partners such as Google, YouTube, and Zapier for embedded media or analytics.

4. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. Without these cookies, basic site capabilities such as security verification, navigation, and preference persistence cannot operate.

  • Purpose: CSRF security protection, user consent state storage, theme persistence.
  • Duration: Up to 1 year or persistent until cleared manually.
  • Opt-Out: Mandatory for platform functionality (cannot be toggled off).

5. Performance Cookies

Performance storage monitors resource loading speeds, script initialization, and user experience metrics across mobile and desktop devices. This helps us optimize asset delivery and reduce latency.

6. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies gather aggregated, non-identifying statistical information regarding how visitors interact with our pages, which learning topics are visited most, and navigation drop-offs.

7. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow HarshGuruJi to provide enhanced features and personalized options, such as saving calculation outputs, bookmarking articles, and retaining active search history filters.

8. Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party integrations embedded on our platform (e.g., YouTube video players, Google Fonts, Zapier GuruAI integration) may set independent cookies when rendered.

9. Google Services Cookies

We utilize Google Fonts and Google Ads infrastructure (`adsbygoogle.js`). Google services may place cookies to serve non-personalized or personalized ads depending on your consent selection and jurisdiction.

10. Firebase Cookies (Future-Ready)

Our platform architecture is built ready for Firebase Authentication and Firestore Database integration. Firebase utilizes persistent authentication state tokens (`indexedDB` and session storage) to maintain secure user login across visits.

11. Authentication Cookies

When you log into your HarshGuruJi account or utilize SSO, secure tokens (`autologinToken`) are stored in client storage to recognize your authenticated session safely.

12. Advertising Cookies

Where advertising is enabled, cookies help measure campaign effectiveness and prevent the same advertisement from showing repeatedly.

13. Embedded Content Cookies

Articles or tools on this website may include embedded content (such as videos, maps, or interactive widgets). Embedded content from third-party websites behaves identically to visiting the external site directly.
