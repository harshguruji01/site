💡 Quick Daily Life Tips

⏰ Time Management

Organize your daily tasks using the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of focused work followed by a 5-minute break.

🧘 Wellness

Start your day with meditation or stretching for just 10 minutes to improve focus and reduce stress.

🎯 Goal Setting

Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) for better success in daily tasks.

🌙 Sleep Hygiene

Maintain a consistent sleep schedule and avoid screens 1 hour before bed for better sleep quality.

🥗 Nutrition

Include a balanced diet with proteins, carbs, and healthy fats to maintain energy throughout the day.

🤝 Social Connection

Spend quality time with friends and family daily. Strong relationships improve mental health and happiness.
